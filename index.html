How do I get Words on Screen™?

Words on Screen™ comes with every purchase of a premium pack product and most are supplied in a CD ROM format, and all accessible online via your Out of the Ark online account. Words on Screen™ online contains extra features to make accessing your music and resources even easier.

If you have purchased a songbook containing Words on Screen™, you will be sent a CD ROM in the post, along with your songbook, musical or nativity.

Simply pop the Words on Screen™ CD in and take advantage of all the features on offer.

Think of this as the digital version of your songbook, musical or nativity – we call this an eSongbook.

This player is online for you to login and listen anytime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Stream your songs. Download your songs. Which one is for you?

When you have logged onto your Out of the Ark account to access your songs, you will be given the option either to stream or download your songs. Here’s a simple explanation to help you work out which one is for you (can this be designed side by side, so you click or roll over one and the info appears – like the 4 tabs on the previous version).

Why stream your songs?

This function gives you the flexibility to log on and access your songs anywhere, on any device, excluding mobile phones, provided you have an internet connection. When you stream your songs every song you have purchased will be visible and playable from your player.

Why download your songs?

If you have an unreliable internet connection in your school, it would be better to download your player to enable you to have complete access to all your purchased songs at any time without the need for internet connection once the download has completed. Note – the player and songs you have downloaded will only be available on the device you have downloaded them to.

Still unsure? Contact our helpful digital team on 0208 941 7200

What is an eSongbook?

An e-songbook is a digital version of your songbook, musical or nativity. It is instant, which means that it will appear as soon as you refresh your player. Your eSongbook contains everything you would typically find in your printed resource. This may be scripts, character lists and prop suggestions for musicals and nativities, or curriculum linked extras and creative ideas for songbooks.

Where can I find my eSongbook?

Once you have a logged into your account page you will be able to access all of your digital resources by either streaming or downloading . When your player is open you will find a link for ‘All eSongbooks’ on the left hand side menu bar. Click on this, and you will see a list of all the e-songbooks you have purchased.

What is in my eSongbook?

Open up your e-songbook by clicking on the title you require. Links to the resources available to you can be found on the left hand side menu bar. Click on any of the links and your resources will be appear in a printable format.

When I click to view my e-songbooks, I can’t seem to use the search bar function.

Don’t worry, we’re aware of this, and are working on a fix. In the meantime if you want to search for a particular song, you need to click on the link in the left hand menu bar labelled ‘All Songs’. This will then display all songs you have in your library. To search for a song, simply start typing the chosen song title in the search bar on the top left of the player screen. Your song will display.
